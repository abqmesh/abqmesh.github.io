Albuquerque Mesh

Albuquerque Mesh Network is a loose knit community of people building a mesh network using Meshtastic radios in Albuquerque, New Mexico. This network acts like a city-wide text messaging system, allowing people to communicate publicly or privately with anyone on the network.

We are just getting started and a lot of details are still being figured out. Stay tuned to this site for more info as things come into better focus.

Friday Mesh-around: Every Friday at 6pm Mountain Time we encourage everyone with a node to reach out on the LongFast channel and say hello. We can stress test the mesh by all hitting it at the same time and see how many contacts we can get. 

In the meantime, consider joining us on the ABQMesh Discord
or email us at info@abqm.net
